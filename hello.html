What is your answer to this math problem?

    This question sparked a debate on twitter. It is a classic case of a problem that does not have one correct answer. Can you see the issue with asking a math problem like this one?

  • The first step is to resolve the bracket, so 2+2 = 4

    • But Now What?

  • You could try 2 x 4 = 8, and then 8/8 would give you one.
  • Or, you could try 8/2 = 4, and then 4 x 2, giving you eight.
